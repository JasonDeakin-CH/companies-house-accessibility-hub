What is accessibility?

The focus of web accessibility is to help all users to perceive, understand, navigate, interact with and contribute to digital services. In other words, the aim is to ensure content is Perceivable, Operable, Understandable and Robust (POUR).

Why should I care about accessibility?

Following user centred design principles that recognise the importance of accessibility is fundamental to creating digital experiences that can be enjoyed by all.
